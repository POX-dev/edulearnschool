Student Activities

Discover your passion, develop new skills, and make lasting friendships through our diverse range of clubs and activities.

At EduLearn K-12, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our extensive selection of clubs, organizations, and activities provides students with opportunities to explore interests, develop leadership skills, and build a vibrant school community. With over 50 active clubs and organizations, there's something for everyone!

How to Join

Most clubs welcome new members throughout the year. Visit the Student Activities Office in the Student Center or contact the club advisor directly to get involved. Club fair is held during the first week of school each year.

Start Your Own Club

Have an idea for a new club? Students can propose new clubs by submitting a club charter to the ASB office. Requirements include a faculty advisor, at least 10 interested members, and a clear mission statement. Contact Mrs. Brown in the Student Center for more information.
